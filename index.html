Ingredients

GRAHAM BALL INGREDIENTS:

FUDGE INGREDIENTS:

  • Marshmallow

PROCEDURE

How to Make Graham Balls with Opuff Marshmallow Centers

1. Prepare the Graham Cracker Mixture

  • In a large mixing bowl, combine the finely crushed graham crackers and condensed milk.

  • Mix until all ingredients are thoroughly combined and form a dough-like consistency.

    • 2. Form the Graham Balls:

  • Take a small amount of the graham cracker mixture and flatten it in your hand.

  • Place an Opuff marshmallow in the center of the flattened mixture.

  • Gently fold the graham cracker mixture around the marshmallow, rolling it into a ball shape, ensuring the marshmallow is fully encased.

    • 3. Chill and Serve:

  • Place the formed graham balls on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

  • Refrigerate for at least 1 hour to allow the graham balls to set.

  • Once set, serve and enjoy the delightful combination of graham cracker, marshmallow, and chocolate in every bite.

    • How to make Graham Ball with Fudgee Bar Flavor and Marshmallow Inside

    1.Prepare the Mixture:

  • In a large mixing bowl, combine the crushed graham, crumbled Fudgee Bars, and sweetened condensed milk.

  • Mix well until all ingredients are thoroughly combined.

    • 2.Form the Balls:

  • Take a small amount of the mixture and flatten it in your hand. Place a marshmallow in the center, then roll the mixture around the marshmallow to form a ball.

  • Repeat this process until all the mixture is used.

    • 3.Coating:

  • Roll each graham ball in graham crushed for an extra taste of texture.

    • 4.Chill and Serve

  • Place the graham balls on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

  • Chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to firm up. Once chilled, they are ready to serve.

    • Enjoy your delicious Graham Balls with Fudgee Bar flavor, a marshmallow and Opuff Marshmallow surprise inside!
